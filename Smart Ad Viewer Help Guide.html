Section One: Setting up Smart Ad Viewer



1. After you have successfully installed, Go to Settings in Menu Bar and click on Add Gmail ID.
(In the Top Left Corner of the Application Window)

2. A small window pops up. Enter your login credentials accordingly and then click on ‘Add Gmail ID’. A notification as ‘New Gmail ID Added!!’ appears in bottom left corner of the same window confirms that the user has been added.

Now you can see the Gmail ID listed in the Connection Section of the Controls Panel on the Left side of the Application Window.

You can add 9 more Gmail IDs following the same (1 & 2) steps.



For all the Gmail IDs you want to add, some settings are to be changed in your Gmail Account as well. Let’s Start:

1. Login to you Gmail Account.

2. On the top right corner of the window click on the round icon (also used for logging out of you Gmail account).

3. Click on the ‘My Account’ button. You will be redirected to your Google Account Section.

4. Click on ‘Signing in to Google’ in the ‘Sign-in & Security’ section.

5. Click on ‘2-step Verification’ in the ‘Password and sign-in method’ section.



6. Click on the ‘Turn Off’ button placed on the right side of the screen. In the pop-up window unmark the ‘Also clear my 2-step verification settings’ option and the click on the ‘Turn Off’ below.

7. Now you can logout of your Google account and return back to the software.



Note: If the ‘2-Step Verification’ is turned off by default then move to Section Two: Using Smart Ad Viewer.









Section Two: Using Smart Ad Viewer



  1. To Start Viewing Ads, Just select the added Gmail ID of your preference in the drop down menu of Users Information, located in the top part of the Controls Panel – on the left side of the application window.

  1. After selecting the Gmail ID, Click on ‘Connect’ below the drop down menu.

  1. When it logs into your Gmail account ‘Connected’ appears. After this the ‘Start Viewing’ button becomes active.




  1. To Start viewing the ads click on ‘Start Viewing’.





  1. Whenever you open Smart Ad Viewer, as default the first ‘Ad Tab’ opens the SafelisTech website. When you click on the ‘Start Viewing’ button the first ad opens in the same ‘Ad Tab’.

After the first ad loads you will also see the bottom bar in the ‘Ad Tab’, where you will find the ‘Know More’ button and the ‘Next’ button.

The ‘Know More’ button opens the ad in your default web browser for you to explore the ad feature.







  1. As a Fair Feature, the ‘Next’ button stays inactive for the first 5 seconds of the ad being displayed. After 5 seconds the ‘Next’ button becomes active for use.

You can also utilize this time to solve any captchas required by the Safelist to earn the credit from the links that are being displayed on the ad feature page.







  1. On clicking ‘Next’ the next ad opens in a new ‘Ad Tab’. Continue to click on ‘Next’ to keep viewing the Ads.



  1. To switch to another Gmail ID, click on the ‘Pause Viewing/Switch Accounts’ button, located right next to ‘Start Viewing’ button.






  2. Select the Gmail ID you wish to switch to and then click ‘Connect’. Following the remaining procedure accordingly.



  1. To activate the ‘Delete Mails’ function, Select the ‘On’ option on the section below the Users Information in the Controls Panel.



  1. To get the latest Inbox Statistics click on the ‘Refresh’ button, located below the box under the ‘Delete Mails’ section.



  1. To remove a Gmail ID, go to ‘Settings’ in the menu bar and select ‘Remove Gmail ID’.

  1. In the pop-up window select the Gmai ID you need to remove and that click on ‘Remove User’.
    A notification as ‘    Gmail ID Removed!!’ appears in bottom left corner of the same window confirms that the user has been removed.









Section Three: Troubleshooting



The Software hangs up or is running slow.

This could be happening due slow internet speed or the system hardware resources being consumed by other processes.



The ‘Next’ button is not active.

This could be due to the latest ‘Ad Tab’ not being selected as the currently active one.



Ad Tab displays ‘Invalid Link’.

Whenever the Ad Tab extracts a credit earning ink that has expired it displays the message Invalid Link.



Software closes after ‘Connect’ or ‘Refresh’ is clicked.

In case the software closes after clicking on the Connect or Refresh button you will need to login to your Gmail account(s) via a web browser.



If the above or any other problems persist, contact us immediately by visiting us at our website.
